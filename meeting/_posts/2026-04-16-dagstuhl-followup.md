### Abstract

This session will serve as a follow up to
 [Dagstuhl Seminar 26022: Program Optimization with E-Graphs](https://www.dagstuhl.de/en/seminars/seminar-calendar/seminar-details/26022)
 which took place in January 2026.

We will have short presentations from participants about their work/directions
 that have come up from the seminar. There will also be time for open discussion.

Anyone is welcome to join, regardless of whether they attended the seminar.
